Summary

David is a seasoned marketer, communications specialist, brand specialist with 20 years of experience, and 4 years as an entrepreneur, co-founder of his boutique agency. David is also a certified WDA corporate trainer and presenting lecturing in ERC Institute specialise in Marketing and Business Management. His vast industries experience ranges from Audio Visual, Automobile , Bathroom Accessories, Education, Entertainment/Broadcast, Fast Moving Consumer Goods, Human Resource, Financial Services, Government, Healthcare , Chemical Industrial, Information Technology, Non-profit Organization , Optical , Postal Services, Property, Pharmaceuticals, Retail, Shipping & Logistic, Tobacco to Telecommunications.

As a brand specialist, he has helped build new brands, re-position existing brands and re-build fading brands. Some of the brands he has helped building including Sony, Volvo, MediaCorp, Kao, Lam Soon, Marigold, Great Eastern Life, Prudential, MasterCard, Templeton, Canon, Microsoft, Haw Par, Djarum and Alcatel to name a few. Drawing on his experience as a co-founder, marketer and brand specialist with fast paced solutions, creative and innovative ideas facilitate him as a dedicated and passionate mentor in new start-ups.

David holds a Master of Science in Business Management majoring in Marketing from Edinburgh Napier University Business School and the Advanced Certificate in Training and Assessment (ACTA) from Singapore Workforce Development Agency.
